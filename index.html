Our Features

Ayurveda Dictionary

In our app, the Ayurveda Dictionary is your comprehensive guide, offering definitions, explanations, and insights into Ayurvedic terminology, herbs, practices, and health principles.

Search Vaidya

"Search Vaidya" expands your Ayurvedic network by enabling you to find nearby practitioners using their pincode, fostering connections and collaborations within the Ayurveda community.

"Ayurveda Resources" is your one-stop hub for comprehensive information on courses, stipend details from various colleges, and online resources in the field of Ayurveda. Access valuable insights to enhance your knowledge and career opportunities in traditional medicine.

Store

"Store" is your destination for customized Ayurveda equipment and raw drugs. Discover high-quality products tailored to your needs, ensuring authenticity and effectiveness in your Ayurvedic practice.

E-Courses

"E-Courses" offers a diverse range of online learning opportunities in Ayurveda. Explore our curated selection of courses, designed to deepen your understanding and skills in traditional medicine from the comfort of your own home.

E-Books

"E-Books" provides a virtual library of invaluable resources in Ayurveda. Access a wide array of electronic books covering topics ranging from herbal remedies to holistic wellness, empowering you with knowledge to support your journey towards optimal health and vitality.
